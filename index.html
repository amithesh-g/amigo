How to Make a Perfect Omelet

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Fillings (cheese, vegetables, ham, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture and cook until edges firm up.
  4. Add fillings to one side and fold the omelet.
  5. Cook for another minute, then serve hot.
